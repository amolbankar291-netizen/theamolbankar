Node structure (used by the ModelWheelRig / FortunerSetup import):
  SUV_Cube            -> body
  SUV_FrontLeftWheel  -> front-left wheel  (steers + spins)
  SUV_FrontRightWheel -> front-right wheel (steers + spins)
  SUV_BackWheels      -> combined rear axle (spins)
